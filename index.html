Research Focus

Can Plants Tell Us When They're Stressed?

Do Moringa oleifera stems emit detectable ultrasonic pops that correlate with water stress and time-of-day state?

Stress-Induced Emissions

Click rate per hour increases under moderate drought stress relative to baseline conditions.

H2

Diurnal Patterns

Click rate exhibits bimodal daily peaks aligned with transpiration windows.
